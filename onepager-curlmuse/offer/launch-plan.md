## Product
- Product: Heatless Satin Curling Set
- Positioning: overnight curls, no heat damage, beginner-friendly

- Demonstration-heavy product (before bed -> morning reveal)
- Strong social content format (short transformation clips)
- Very giftable and high repeat intent (friends/sisters/moms)
- Clear bundle logic that boosts AOV quickly

4. Benefits: less heat, less time, softer feel
5. Bundle pricing cards
6. FAQ + guarantee
