# Marketing

## Core Workflow

1. Define objective, audience, and offer.
2. Build positioning and messaging.
3. Select channel strategy and campaign plan.
4. Define funnel, creative briefs, and execution cadence.
5. Measure performance and iterate.

## 1) Define Objective and Audience

Set:
- business goal
- target audience segments
- desired action
- timeline and budget constraints

Prefer one primary KPI and a small set of supporting KPIs.

## 2) Build Positioning and Messaging

Define:
- value proposition
- proof points
- objections and responses
- message hierarchy by audience intent

Keep claims specific and defensible.

## 3) Select Channel and Campaign Plan

Choose channels based on audience behavior and unit economics.
For each channel, define:
- role in funnel (awareness, consideration, conversion, retention)
- content/ad format
- budget split
- launch sequence and cadence

## 4) Define Execution Artifacts

Prepare:
- campaign brief
- channel-specific creative briefs
- landing page requirements
- email/nurture flow outline
- experiment backlog with hypotheses

## 5) Measure and Optimize

Track:
- traffic quality
- conversion rates by step
- CAC, payback, and retention signal
- creative/message performance by segment

Use weekly iteration loops and make one major variable change at a time.

## Output Standard

When responding:
1. State assumptions and target segment.
2. Provide campaign plan by channel.
3. Include messaging framework and examples.
4. Define KPI table and reporting cadence.
5. End with risks and next experiments.
