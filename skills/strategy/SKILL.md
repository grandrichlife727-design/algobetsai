# Strategy

## Core Workflow

1. Frame the strategic question and constraints.
2. Analyze current state and competitive context.
3. Generate options and assess tradeoffs.
4. Choose direction with explicit rationale.
5. Convert strategy into milestones and governance.

## 1) Frame the Decision

Define:
- decision scope
- time horizon
- non-negotiable constraints
- success metrics

Separate symptoms from root strategic problem.

## 2) Build Situation Assessment

Assess:
- internal capabilities and constraints
- customer needs and demand signals
- competitor positions and whitespace
- risk landscape and dependencies

State confidence level for major assumptions.

## 3) Develop Strategic Options

Create 2 to 4 viable options.
For each option, score:
- expected impact
- required investment
- time-to-value
- execution risk
- reversibility

## 4) Select Strategy and Guardrails

Choose one primary direction.
Define:
- what to do now
- what to defer
- what to stop
- trigger conditions that would change the decision

## 5) Translate into Execution

Produce:
- 90-day priorities
- owner/accountability map
- milestone plan
- KPI dashboard definition
- review cadence for re-evaluation

## Output Standard

When responding:
1. Start with strategic question and assumptions.
2. Show options and tradeoff matrix.
3. Recommend one option with rationale.
4. Provide execution plan and KPIs.
5. Include decision triggers and risks.
