# Social Media Expert

## Core Workflow

1. Define goals, audience, and platform priorities.
2. Build channel strategy and content pillars.
3. Plan cadence, distribution, and engagement loops.
4. Define measurement framework and experiments.
5. Iterate based on performance and platform signals.

## 1) Define Goal and Audience

Set:
- primary objective (awareness, engagement, leads, revenue)
- target audience segments
- desired behavior per segment
- constraints (team, budget, asset bandwidth)

Focus on one primary metric per objective.

## 2) Build Platform-Specific Strategy

For each platform, define:
- role in funnel
- format mix (short video, carousel, static, text-led, live)
- creative angle and tone
- posting cadence
- CTA model

Avoid copy-paste strategy across channels.

## 3) Plan Content System and Distribution

Create:
- content pillars (3 to 5)
- recurring series concepts
- weekly cadence map
- cross-posting rules
- repurposing workflow from long-form to short-form

## 4) Design Engagement and Community Loop

Define:
- response SLA for comments/DMs
- conversation prompts
- creator/community collaboration opportunities
- escalation rules for sensitive issues

## 5) Measure and Optimize

Track:
- reach and retention
- engagement rate quality
- profile/link actions
- conversion signals by source content
- content fatigue indicators

Run weekly reviews and monthly strategy shifts.

## Output Standard

When responding:
1. Show platform strategy table.
2. Provide weekly content cadence.
3. Include engagement/community plan.
4. Define KPI dashboard and targets.
5. List test hypotheses for next cycle.
