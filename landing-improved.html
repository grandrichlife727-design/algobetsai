FAQ

Good questions.

Is this a tout service? Are picks guaranteed? ⌄
No picks are ever guaranteed — anyone who says otherwise is lying to you. Algobets Ai is a data and analytics tool that surfaces statistical edges. You make your own decisions. The goal is positive expected value over a large sample, not a win on every bet.
Where does the odds data come from? ⌄
We pull live odds from The Odds API, which aggregates data from 40+ bookmakers including DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, and more. Data is fetched in real-time on every scan. No stale or simulated odds.
What sports do you cover? ⌄
NBA, NFL, NHL, NCAAB, and MLB. We prioritize markets with the most liquidity and sharpest lines — these are where edges are real but also where they can be found if you have the right tools.
Does it work on mobile? ⌄
Algobets Ai is a web app that works perfectly on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. On iPhone, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen" and it will look and behave like a native app. No App Store download required.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ⌄
Yes. Cancel at any time from your Stripe billing portal — no questions asked, no cancellation fees. Your access continues through the end of your billing period.
What's the backtested win rate? ⌄
