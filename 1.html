How It Works
Feature guide, system overview, and practical workflow
Start Here (Daily Workflow)
1) Run Scan 2) Check +EV and Odds 3) Size bets with bankroll rules 4) Track outcomes.
Best practice: skip forced bets when the board is thin. Use Tomorrow’s Games to plan ahead.
Core Tabs
Picks: ranked opportunities with edge and confidence.
Odds: book-by-book moneylines (Today/Tomorrow/Week).
+EV: opportunities priced above fair value.
Steam/Fades: market pressure and contrarian spots.
How Live Picks Work
Live mode focuses on games near start or in-play windows where lines can move quickly.
Use it to monitor timing-sensitive spots, then confirm current odds before placing.
If a price moves against you, the edge may be gone. Re-check before entry.
How +EV Works
+EV compares best available book price to estimated fair probability (after de-vig).
Higher +EV means better long-run value, not guaranteed short-term wins.
Prioritize stronger EV with acceptable confidence and line stability.
How Steam Works
Steam flags fast market movement and book divergence that can indicate sharp pressure.
Best use: identify where price is likely to keep moving, then act before it closes.
Premium users get full steam access and faster refresh windows.
How Fades Work
Fades surface situations where public ticket flow is heavy on one side.
When pricing still favors the opposite side, contrarian value can appear.
Fade signals are strongest when supported by EV and line/steam context.
How Props Work
Props Edge scans selected player markets and shows best available price/book.
Use props as single positions or pair with straight bets only when value supports it.
Props are budget-guarded to keep API spend controlled and data quality stable.
5-Agent System (Simple)
Best-Line EV: compares best available line vs fair probability.
De-vig Consensus: removes juice to estimate true market baseline.
Sharp-Book Weighting: prioritizes stronger market-making books.
Steam Signal: flags meaningful line divergence/movement.
Public Fades: highlights one-sided public ticket pressure.
Free vs Premium
Free: lighter access and limited pick volume.
Premium: higher pick limits, full +EV, Props Edge, steam, and fades.
Use monthly for flexibility; annual for lower effective monthly cost.
Bankroll + Risk
Use unit sizing (typically 0.5% to 2% per bet) and daily stop-loss limits.
Do not chase losses. Treat this as long-horizon expected value.
No pick is guaranteed. The edge comes from price discipline over sample size.
Important Notes
If live data is unavailable, the app can show cached/demo states.
Line movement can invalidate edge quickly. Re-check odds before placing.
21+ only. Bet responsibly.
